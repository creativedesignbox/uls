ULTRA X6000

The ULTRA X6000 is a high-precision laser material processing system designed for demanding and complex applications in manufacturing, prototyping, and research and development environments.

Key Features and Capabilities

  • 36 x 24 in. (914 mm x 610 mm) Material Processing Area
  • Supports material thickness up to 12 in. (305 mm)
  • Supports three wavelengths - 9.3 µm CO2, 10.6 µm CO2 and 1.06 µm Fiber
Learn More

ULTRA R9000

The ULTRA R9000 is the largest laser system in the ULTRA series with a 48 in x 24 in (1219 mm x 610 mm) material processing area.

Key Features and Capabilities

  • Material processing area of 48 x 24 in. (1219 mm x 610 mm)
  • Supports material thickness up to 12 in. (305 mm)
  • Supports three wavelengths - 9.3 µm CO2, 10.6 µm CO2 and 1.06 µm Fiber

ULTRA R5000
